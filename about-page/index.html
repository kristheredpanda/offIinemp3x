About OfflineMP3X

The idea of OfflineMP3X came around to my mind when I wanted to listen to music on a network where Spotify or YouTube was restricted, so I began creating, not having a plan, so I just winged it to be honest.

This program however was not my first attempt at an MP3 player for Windows, in fact, I had created many other variations of such a thing and gave up each time, eventually building up the knowledge I needed to make this. I decided that it'd be a good idea to make this website so that my creation can be shared with others and hopefully become a tool many others use.
